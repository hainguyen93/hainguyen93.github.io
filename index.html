research interests

My research interests are in Black-Box Optimisation, Evolutionary Computation and Artificial Intelligence. I am currently working on the class of model-based algorithms, i.e. Estimation of Distribution Algorithms, which iteratively update a probability distribution defined on a search space in order to optimise an objective function. The algorithm iteratively fits a probability distribution to a batch of elite individuals in the population using Maximum Likelihood.

My primary supervisor is Dr. Per Kristian Lehre, and my secondary supervisor is Prof. Xin Yao. My thesis group members are Prof. Jon Rowe and Prof. Ata Kaban (RSMG rep).
